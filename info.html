Welcome to En Avant Ballet School

En Avant Ballet School was established in 1992 in North London with the aim of providing continued support for dancers, from the very first steps into ballet and throughout their careers.

We are more than a school, we've become a multigenerational family, with two of our present teachers having started their ballet journey with us, more than 20 years ago, and now training the next generation of dancers and teachers.

Our teachers are professional dancers, they have been subject to DBS checks, and are trained in Safeguarding Policy.

At En Avant we address the three main components of a complete dancer: artistic, technical and mental. All our lessons have piano accompaniment for enhanced musicality, inspiration and atmosphere. Our teachers strive to instill the correct technical skills to avoid injuries and ensure smooth progress. Our mental trainers collaborate with our teachers to insure the best mental preparation techniques are embedded in the day-to-day lessons.

We motivate our students to set hight targets and always take one step further: En Avant!
